About

VEAPtv is an optimized video streaming platform powered by the Tagline Group of Companies and Binary Digital Advertising. An add-on to VEAP (Virtual Events & Activations Platform), it is envisioned as an impactful and cost-efficient solution to businesses with emergent digital requirements, with the goal of expanding their audience engagement even further.

It is offered in two different versions.
Standard

A standalone Video Event Page on a separate VEAP.tv website. Here, viewers can access the Event page on the same website.

Supplement

An add-on to the Plenary function of VEAP, allowing users with intermittent Internet connections to access the platform.
