Features

Live Video

Broadcasting first via StreamYard, your live video will then be mirrored to a Third Party Video Platform of your choice, which can be YouTube or Facebook. Live Engagements or audience interaction will then be made possible by Q&A and polling platform Slido.

Customized Branding

As a commercial livestreaming platform, VEAP TV allows you to customize the interface to fit your branding requirements. Upload your own materials, or use our FREE VEAP.tv template!

Info Page

Let your audience know what your event is all about on the Info Page, where you can input descriptions and other important details.

Media Page

Upload media crucial to your event, product, or service. This can be in the form of documents, links, photos, videos, and other formats.

Surveys

Gauge audience feedback by conducting surveys after your live video event.

Ad Placements

VEAP TV also offers additional monetization opportunities through targeted ad placements, allowing you to empower your event sponsors during the event as well.
